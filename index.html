Waterfalls Tours

The mysterious and deceitful Davis Fall - locally known as 'Patale Chhango', which means 'The Hell's Fall'! The modern name 'Davis Fall' was derived from the tragic incident of a foreigner named Davis who got swept into the underground passage by the strong currents never to be seen again!

Events And Festivals

The biggest and most popular festivals are Dashain and Tihar. Despite their religious beliefs, people of every ethnicity celebrate Dashain and Tihar with the same joy and excitement. The two festivals fall in the autumn season and are observed as the longest festival holiday
