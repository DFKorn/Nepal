Pokhara

Standing on top of the Anadu Hill, World Peace Pagoda (Shanti Stupa) adds beauty to the Pokhara Valley. It is a perfect holiday place providing a clear view of the Himalayan range, Phewa Lake and Pokhara city. The hilltop provides a splendid view of sunrise and sunset.
